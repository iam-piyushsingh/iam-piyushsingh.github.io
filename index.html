Class 8 🏆 1st Prize

Anti-Sleep Alarm System for Vehicle Drivers

A safety device that detects driver drowsiness using an infrared eye-blink sensor. When the driver's eyes remain closed beyond a threshold, the circuit triggers a loud buzzer and flashing LED — alerting the driver before an accident occurs. An affordable, life-saving innovation built entirely from scratch.

Class 7 🏆 1st Prize

LPG Gas Detector

A home safety device that continuously monitors the air for LPG gas leakage. Using an MQ-6 gas sensor, the circuit detects dangerous gas concentrations and instantly triggers a red LED alert and buzzer alarm — preventing potential fire or explosion hazards. A critical safety tool for every kitchen.

Class 6 🏆 1st Prize

Solar Tracker

An automatic solar panel tracking system that follows the sun's position throughout the day using light-dependent resistors (LDRs) and a servo motor. By always pointing at the strongest light source, the tracker maximises solar energy collection — demonstrating renewable energy principles in a hands-on, working model.
